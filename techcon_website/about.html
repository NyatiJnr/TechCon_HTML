History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2005 as a small gathering of tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley, aiming to foster innovation and collaboration. Over the years, it has grown into a globally recognized conference, hosting thousands of attendees from diverse tech fields. From its humble beginnings in a community center to its current status at the San Francisco Convention Center, TechCon has been a platform for groundbreaking ideas and networking.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 is dedicated to advancing technology for a better future. Our mission is to connect innovators, share knowledge, and inspire solutions to global challenges. We strive to create an inclusive environment where diverse voices in tech can collaborate and thrive.
